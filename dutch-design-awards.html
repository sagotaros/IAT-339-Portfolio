Dutch Design Awards

Introduction

This is an art direction + interaction design project envisioning an expressive post-event microsite hosted by Dutch Design Awards. Featuring innovative and impactful works from Dutch designers across various disciplines.

My Role

Interaction Design, Art Direction, Visual Design

Team

Tool

Duration

Jan 25 - Feb 29, 2024 (1 month)

Microsite Design

This microsite is set after the end of the 2023 Dutch Design Awards show and serves to showcase the projects from the winners of the 8 different categories. Users can use the site to achieve 2 goals:

1. Highlight winners from nominees

The current Dutch Design Awards website organizes both winner and nominees together by the award categories, making it difficult for users to see only the winners.

2. Understand the designer’s creative process

Users unfamiliar with the project can fully understand the designer’s creative process from their research to solution for the proposed work.

1. Landing Page

When the users scroll down the gifs of different designers' work process with float through the typography and into the mission statement of the site. Then Users can select one of the designers to see their content pages. When the user hovers over the designer's name, quick previews of images showcasing the designer in action will be displayed. We showcase brief previews of images to provide an insight into the designers' creative processes.

2. Video Player

When the users land in the content page, a video of their work process will be shown in a video player designed to fit the art direction of the site. The video is segmented into distinctly labeled sections, allowing the viewer to easily identify and directly access specific stages of the designer's process.

3. Content Page

Closeup shots are present to demonstrates the techniques the designer’s use in their process, as well as quotes to serve as the most effective means to encapsulate their process and offer insights into their creative perspectives. At the end of the process is a gallery featuring all the photos of the designers process, aiming to spotlight every aspect of their journey.

Graphic Experimentation
