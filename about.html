About Me

Hello! I am Jimmy, currently a student studying at the Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) program of SFU with a concentration on design. Through the program I was able to learn to use digital softwares such as Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud applications, and more to indulge in my creative projects. I was also introduced to various coding languages such as Python, HTML, CSS, Java, and JavaScript which I had used to create websites including my own portfolio!

Onto my personal hobbies, I've always had a keen interest in Japanese Animation which is what ignited my love for illustrative work which I have taught myself to use digital art softwares such as Clip Studio Paint to create artworks not only as a hobby but as a freelance illustrator online.
